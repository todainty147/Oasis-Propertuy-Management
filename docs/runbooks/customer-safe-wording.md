# Customer-Safe Support Wording

Use these phrases when replying to support tickets involving tax, compliance evidence, RPE, monitoring, billing, provenance, documents, or AI. Keep wording factual, evidence-based, and non-certifying.

## General principles

- Say what Tenaqo recorded, checked, or can trace.
- Say when a result is demo-mode, internal-only, sandbox, pilot-gated, or review-required.
- Avoid certifying legal, tax, security, or financial outcomes.
- Avoid implying that automated monitoring replaces expert review.
- Escalate rather than improvise when evidence is incomplete.

## HMRC / MTD

Use:

“Tenaqo can prepare and evidence a reviewed HMRC MTD draft. Sandbox and dry-run checks do not file a real return.”

Avoid:

Claims that Tenaqo certifies tax accuracy, replaces an accountant, or files live returns outside the controlled pilot path.

## RPE / proof packs

Use:

“The proof pack shows the evidence state Tenaqo can trace from reviewed rules and recorded data. Items marked review-required need human review.”

Avoid:

Claims that a proof pack is a final legal determination or removes all risk.

## Regulatory monitoring

Use:

“Tenaqo tracks selected sources and can flag detected changes for internal review. Detected changes are not applied to customer obligations until reviewed and approved.”

Avoid:

Claims that Tenaqo watches every legal source or automatically updates obligations from an unreviewed source change.

## Provenance / finance evidence

Use:

“We are checking the recorded evidence and preserving the audit trail. If the evidence basis differs from a previous formula, we show that as a review item.”

Avoid:

Claims that a balance is legally recoverable solely because it appears in the app.

## Billing / entitlement

Use:

“We are checking the account’s billing and entitlement state against Stripe and Tenaqo’s feature resolver.”

Avoid:

Claims that a plan or entitlement grants compliance, tax, or legal readiness.

## Auth / account isolation

Use:

“We are checking the account membership and role permissions for the account you are trying to access. We will not bypass account isolation.”

Avoid:

Offering to expose data from another account or bypass role restrictions.

## AI features

Use:

“The AI feature provides an assistance signal based on available records. If source data is missing or the provider is unavailable, the result may be limited.”

Avoid:

Presenting AI output as professional advice or as a substitute for source evidence.
