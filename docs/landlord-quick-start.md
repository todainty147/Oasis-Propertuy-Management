# OASIS landlord quick start

OASIS helps you run the day-to-day work around your portfolio in one place: properties, tenants, rent, maintenance, contractors, and shared documents.

## Start here

1. Add your first property  
   Go to `Properties` and add the address, city, and unit details that make up your portfolio.

2. Invite your first tenant  
   Go to `Invitations` and send a tenant invite. Once they join, they can view their payments, report issues, and access shared information tied to their account scope.

3. Track rent and payments  
   Open `Finance` to review paid, due, and overdue balances. You can also record payments from the same area when your role allows it.

4. Stay on top of maintenance  
   Use `Maintenance Inbox` to review incoming issues, assign work, and keep requests moving.

5. Invite contractors when you need outside help  
   Contractors can use their portal to see assigned jobs and update progress. Quote and invoice steps appear where that workflow is enabled.

## What the dashboard shows

Your dashboard is the fastest way to understand what needs attention right now:

- occupied vs vacant units
- overdue and due-soon rent
- open maintenance pressure
- quick actions into finance, maintenance, and portfolio health

## A simple first-week setup

- Add every active property first.
- Invite tenants for the units that are already occupied.
- Review the Finance page to make sure upcoming rent is visible.
- Open the maintenance inbox once so you know where new issues will land.
- Invite at least one contractor before your first urgent repair.
