# Tenaqo MTD ITSA roadmap

Tenaqo's immediate target is Phase E1: Full UK Property End-to-End Compliance. Current scope is in-year UK property digital records and quarterly update workflows only.

| Phase | Scope | Status |
| --- | --- | --- |
| E1 | Full UK Property End-to-End Compliance | Immediate target |
| E2 | UK Property Year-End | Future |
| E3 | Foreign Property | Future |
| E4 | Self-Employment | Future |
| E5 | Full Tax Calculation and Final Declaration | Future |

Tenaqo must not claim full MTD ITSA coverage. Final declarations, annual adjustments, foreign property, self-employment, partnerships, full tax calculation, and full Self Assessment filing are not supported in E1.

`READY_FOR_GENERAL_LIVE_SUBMISSION` remains `false`. Production access is pending HMRC approval and no landlord-facing live submit control is available.
