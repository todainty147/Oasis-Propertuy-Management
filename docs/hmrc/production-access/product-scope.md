# Product scope

Current HMRC scope: in-year UK property digital records and quarterly update workflows. Production access is pending HMRC approval.

E1 includes Business Details, obligations, UK Property reads, quarterly preparation/submission/amendment controls, fraud headers, consent, audit, errors, and export. It excludes final declaration, year-end adjustments, foreign property, self-employment, partnerships, full tax calculation, penalties exposure, and full Self Assessment filing.

Tenaqo does not provide tax advice and is not described as HMRC approved or accredited. `READY_FOR_GENERAL_LIVE_SUBMISSION` is `false`.
