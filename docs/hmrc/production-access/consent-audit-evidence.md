# Consent, audit and liability evidence

Consent is explicit, append-only, and bound to a locked draft through hashes of draft lines, category totals, validation summary, payload preview, timestamps, and user/account identity. Any change makes consent stale. Amendment drafts require fresh consent.

The UI states that this is a quarterly update only, not a final declaration or full tax return; Tenaqo does not provide tax advice; the user remains responsible for figures; and submission is based on reviewed digital records.

Managers can read safe account audit summaries. Tenant and contractor roles receive no access to HMRC submission, pilot evidence, or consent records. Duplicate guards remain active.
