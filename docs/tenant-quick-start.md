# OASIS tenant quick start

OASIS helps you keep up with rent, maintenance, and shared property information in one place.

## Start here

1. Accept your invite  
   Use the invite link you received to join the correct property account.

2. Check your payments  
   Open `Payments` to see what is paid, due soon, or overdue.

3. Report maintenance issues  
   Use the property and maintenance areas to raise issues and follow updates.

4. Review shared documents when available  
   Your landlord may share documents such as agreements, notices, or related files through the app.

## Good to know

- You will only see your own tenant-scoped information.
- If something looks missing, it is usually a permissions or invite-account issue rather than a deleted record.
- For payment questions or missing access, contact your landlord or property team first.
