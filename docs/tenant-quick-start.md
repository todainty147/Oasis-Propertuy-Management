# OASIS tenant quick start

OASIS helps you keep up with rent, maintenance, documents, and shared property information in one place.

## Start here

1. Accept your invite  
   Use the invite link you received to join the correct property account.

2. Start from `Dashboard`  
   Your home overview shows what needs attention, recent updates, payment summary, available documents, and current maintenance activity.

3. Check your payments  
   Open `Payments` to see what is paid, what remains open, and anything that needs review.

4. Report and track maintenance issues  
   Use the property and maintenance areas to raise issues and follow updates on who currently has the baton.

5. Review shared documents  
   Open `Documents` to see files shared with you. Some documents may be marked as current or needing attention.

6. Check your timeline  
   The tenant timeline shows recent updates related to your tenancy, payments, requests, and documents.

## Good to know

- You will only see your own tenant-scoped information.
- If something looks missing, it is usually a permissions, invite-account, or property-linking issue rather than a deleted record.
- For payment questions or missing access, contact your landlord or property team first.
