# OASIS contractor quick start

OASIS gives you a focused view of the jobs assigned to you.

## Start here

1. Open the contractor portal  
   You will only see work orders assigned to your account and user.

2. Review the job details  
   Open a job to check the property, issue summary, notes, and scheduling details.

3. Keep the status current  
   Update the job status as work moves from assigned to in progress or complete.

4. Submit quotes or invoices when requested  
   If the financial workflow is enabled for the job, you can submit a quote draft, send it for review, and later upload invoice details after approval.

## Good to know

- You do not need to search the whole portfolio. OASIS only shows the jobs scoped to you.
- If a job is missing, it usually means it has not been assigned to your user yet.
- If you cannot update a job, ask the landlord or staff team to confirm the assignment and workflow state.
