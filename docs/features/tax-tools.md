# Tax Tools

## Purpose

Tax Tools organise landlord records for accountant review. They do not replace tax advice and they do not submit anything to HMRC.

## Current Capability

- Tax Calendar
- Records
- MTD Expense Tracker
- Quarterly Drafts
- Section 24 Finance Cost Tracker
- Carried-Forward Finance Costs
- Digital Record Readiness
- Export / Accountant Pack

## Quarterly Draft Flow

Quarterly Drafts reuse existing Tenaqo records instead of creating a new tax ledger. Draft lines preserve source references, category mapping, issue status and included/excluded state.

Review-only records such as capital improvements, mixed-use expenses, finance costs and needs-accountant-review items are visible in the draft but excluded from totals until deliberately included.

## Readiness

Digital Record Readiness is a record-keeping score. It is not a full MTD compliance score. The readiness UI explains the score and reminds users to create/review quarterly drafts before relying on accountant-pack exports.

## Exports

Exports are for review. They include timestamps and disclaimers and must not include HMRC tokens, refresh tokens, client secrets or unnecessary identifiers.

## Not Implemented

- HMRC sandbox quarterly submission
- HMRC live quarterly submission
- Final declaration
- Tax advice
